SURVEY SPLIT / MOVE

This dialog is opened from the Split/Move button of the shot secondary edit dialog.

You can split the current survey and create a new survey. The the shots for the one being edited included to the end are moved to the new survey.
Alternately you can move the shots to an already existing survey. For this there is a selector to choose the survey to which the shots are moved.
Buttons:

  • Split split the shots into a new survey
  • Move move the shots to the selected survey
  • Cancel closes the dialog without doing anything
